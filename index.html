About Us

Helios Voltaics, Inc. is a Florida-based solar labor subcontracting company built by seasoned professionals with over 10 years of experience in the solar and electrical industry. Founded by Alexander Bello and Rafael Nuccio, our mission is to provide licensed solar contractors (CVC) and electrical contractors (EC) with highly skilled, safety-certified labor teams who deliver exceptional results—on time, every time.Let us power your projects—reliably and efficiently.

  • Battery and inverter swaps
  • EV Charger Installations
  • Main Panel Upgrades (MPUs)

  • Trenching & Meter Swaps
